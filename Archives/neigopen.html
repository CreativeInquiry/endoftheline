Neighborhood Views

Youthbuild, Pittsburgh, Inc.
 The neighborhoods of Pittsburgh are rich in architectural details, community projects and interesting people. The exciting thing about this art project was getting to spend time in Pittsburgh neighborhoods, listening to amazing stories of growing up in Pittsburgh often from people whose families have lived here for generations. For more in depth neighborhood histories, you can visit Bridging the Urban Landscape, a project of the Pennsylvania Department at the Carnegie Library. Barry Chad, assistant director of the Pennsylvania Department and creator of the site, was one of our collaborators on this project.
